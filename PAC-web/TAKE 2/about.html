About Priority Activator Consulting

Advisory Built for
African Realities

We partner with organizations to navigate complexity, strengthen performance, and create measurable impact through integrated advisory expertise spanning business, digital, development, and human capital.

Company Overview

Priority Activator Consulting (PAC) was founded to address a critical gap in the advisory landscape: organizations across Africa need partners who understand local context while applying global best practices. We bridge that divide.

Our team brings together expertise across business strategy, digital transformation, international development, and human capital management. We have delivered over 150 engagements across 12 African countries, working with governments, development agencies, private enterprises, and civil society organizations.

We bridge the gap between global best practice and local context, delivering advisory that creates lasting impact across Africa.

Our Mission

To empower organizations across Africa with the strategic insight, operational capability, and human capital needed to achieve sustainable growth and meaningful impact.

We believe that lasting change comes from building local capacity, not dependency. Every engagement is designed to strengthen the institutions and people we work with, leaving them better equipped to face future challenges independently.

Our Vision

A thriving Africa powered by capable institutions, innovative enterprises, and empowered people. We envision a continent where organizations have the strategic depth, technical expertise, and human talent to solve their own challenges and drive inclusive prosperity.

PAC aims to be the most trusted advisory partner for organizations committed to making a difference across the continent, known for the quality of our insight, the practicality of our solutions, and the lasting impact of our work.
