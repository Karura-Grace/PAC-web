1. Program Design & Implementation

  • Design high-impact programs aligned with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)
  • Develop contextualized theories of change and results frameworks
  • Provide end-to-end project planning and implementation support
  • Offer technical expertise in youth empowerment, public health, financial inclusion, and governance
  • Create adaptive program management systems
  • Ensure programs deliver measurable social impact

2. Monitoring & Evaluation (MEAL)

  • Develop comprehensive MEAL systems for evidence-based decision-making
  • Design monitoring tools and data collection frameworks
  • Conduct baseline, midline, and endline surveys with rigorous methodology
  • Execute impact evaluations using mixed-method approaches
  • Promote adaptive learning and embed knowledge-sharing frameworks
  • Build organizational capacity for continuous program improvement

3. Research & Policy Analysis

  • Conduct rigorous socioeconomic, gender, and governance assessments
  • Execute participatory community research and rapid appraisals
  • Generate evidence through quantitative and qualitative research methods
  • Translate research findings into actionable policy insights
  • Provide targeted policy analysis and development support
  • Support evidence-based advocacy and decision-making

  • Facilitate strategic planning processes for NGOs, CSOs, SACCOs, and public institutions
  • Help organizations clarify vision, mission, and strategic priorities
  • Conduct organizational capacity assessments (OCAs) and gap analyses
  • Develop institutional development strategies and implementation plans
  • Provide governance and board advisory services
  • Implement systems improvements for enhanced operational performance

5. Advocacy & Stakeholder Engagement

  • Design effective advocacy strategies that amplify voices and influence policy
  • Conduct comprehensive stakeholder mapping and engagement planning
  • Develop and execute awareness campaigns and policy dialogues
  • Build multi-sectoral coalitions and partnerships
  • Empower youth and women through participatory advocacy frameworks
  • Facilitate community engagement and citizen participation initiatives

6. Grant Management & Resource Mobilization

  • Strengthen organizational ability to secure and manage funding
  • Provide expert proposal writing and donor engagement strategies

  • Develop sustainable financing strategies and diversified funding portfolios

  • Offer training in governance, project management, M&E, and leadership
