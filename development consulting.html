In Depth

What We Actually Do

Concrete activities across every development consulting engagement so you know exactly what you get.

Program Design and Implementation

We design high impact programs aligned with the SDGs, using strong theories of change and results frameworks that ensure every intervention delivers measurable social impact.

  • Theory of change and results frameworks
  • Project planning and adaptive management
  • Technical expertise in youth, health, finance
  • Co create theories of change with stakeholders
  • Develop contextualized results frameworks
  • Provide end to end project planning and support
  • Adaptive management for changing contexts

Monitoring, Evaluation and Learning

Comprehensive MEAL systems that generate evidence for decision making, learning, and accountability.

  • Design monitoring tools and data frameworks
  • Conduct baseline, midline, and endline surveys
  • Mixed method impact evaluations
  • Embed knowledge sharing and adaptive learning

Research and Policy Analysis

Rigorous research that translates into actionable policy insights and evidence based advocacy.

  • Socioeconomic, gender, and governance assessments
  • Participatory community research and appraisals
  • Quantitative and qualitative research methods
  • Policy briefs and advocacy support

Strategic Planning and Institutional Strengthening

We help organizations clarify their vision, mission, and strategic priorities, and build the systems to achieve them.

  • Facilitate strategic planning processes
  • Conduct organizational capacity assessments
  • Develop institutional development strategies
  • Governance and board advisory services

Advocacy, Stakeholder Engagement and Communications

Design advocacy strategies that amplify voices, influence policy, and build broad coalitions for change, backed by tailored training and strategic communications that drive behavioral change and institutional growth.

  • Stakeholder mapping and engagement planning
  • Develop and execute awareness campaigns
  • Build multi sectoral coalitions and partnerships
  • Empower youth and women through participatory advocacy
  • Training in governance, MandE, leadership
  • Blended learning and mentorship
  • Strategic communication, knowledge products and digital storytelling
